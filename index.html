Features

Expense Logging 🧾

Track every rupee (or dollar) you spend—instantly. Easily add daily transactions, categorize them (food, transport, bills, etc.), and attach notes or receipts for future reference.

Smart Budgeting 📊

Set monthly or weekly budgets across different categories. Get visual feedback when you’re approaching or exceeding limits, and learn where you can cut back or reallocate your funds.

Visual Insights & Analytics 📈

View your financial data through interactive charts and graphs. See where your money goes, track trends, identify spending patterns, and make informed decisions to improve your financial well-being.

Alerts & Reminders 🔔

Never miss a bill or budget update again. Get automatic reminders for upcoming payments, low balances, or overspending warnings directly to your dashboard or email.

Secure & Private 🔐

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Only you have access to your personal financial history and information—your privacy is our priority.

Mobile-Friendly Interface 📱

Track your expenses on the go with a responsive design that works across all devices—phones, tablets, and desktops.
