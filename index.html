Take Control of Your Spending!

Effortlessly manage your finances with our all-in-one expense tracker. Monitor your daily spending, create custom budgets, and get smart insights to improve your financial habits. Whether you're saving for a goal or just trying to stay on top of expenses, our app is designed to make money management simple & clear.

Features

Expense Logging 🧾

Track every rupee (or dollar) you spend—instantly. Easily add daily transactions, categorize them (food, transport, bills, etc.), and attach notes or receipts for future reference.

Smart Budgeting 📊

Set monthly or weekly budgets across different categories. Get visual feedback when you’re approaching or exceeding limits, and learn where you can cut back or reallocate your funds.

Visual Insights & Analytics 📈

View your financial data through interactive charts and graphs. See where your money goes, track trends, identify spending patterns, and make informed decisions to improve your financial well-being.

Alerts & Reminders 🔔

Never miss a bill or budget update again. Get automatic reminders for upcoming payments, low balances, or overspending warnings directly to your dashboard or email.

Secure & Private 🔐

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Only you have access to your personal financial history and information—your privacy is our priority.

Mobile-Friendly Interface 📱

Track your expenses on the go with a responsive design that works across all devices—phones, tablets, and desktops.

FAQs

What is this eTrackr?

It's a simple yet powerful tool to help you track your daily spending, set budgets, and stay in control of your finances.

How does it help with budgeting?

You can set monthly or category-wise budgets and get alerts when you’re about to exceed them.

Is this app free to use?

Yes! It's completely free to use with all core features available to everyone.

Can I use it on my phone?

Absolutely. The app is fully responsive and works great on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

What types of expenses can I track?

You can track anything—from groceries and rent to subscriptions, travel, and custom categories.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is stored securely using modern encryption practices to ensure privacy and safety.

Can I export my data?

We're currently working on adding export options for PDF and Excel. Stay tuned—it's coming soon!

Do I need to create an account?

You cannot use it without an account as creating one lets us back up your data and sync across devices.

Can I manage multiple budgets?

Yes, you can create and manage separate budgets for different goals or categories.

How do I get started?

Just sign up or open the app, and start by adding your first expense—it’s that easy!
